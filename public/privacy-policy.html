Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

At Midasbuy ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services to purchase gaming currency including PUBG Mobile UC, Free Fire Diamonds, BGMI UC, and other digital gaming products.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Midasbuy services, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password, and profile details
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address, and payment method preferences
  • Gaming Information: Player IDs, game usernames, server selection, and game account details
  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number when you contact support or subscribe to newsletters
  • Transaction History: Purchase records, order details, and transaction amounts

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referral sources
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session data, preferences, and analytics information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To fulfill your purchases of UC, diamonds, and gaming currency
  • Account Management: To create and manage your Midasbuy account
  • Payment Processing: To securely process transactions and prevent fraud
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries, resolve issues, and provide assistance
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our platform
  • Marketing Communications: To send promotional offers, updates, and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and security breaches
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal, and other payment gateways to process transactions
  • Game Publishers: Tencent, Garena, and other official partners for currency delivery
  • Cloud Services: Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud for data storage
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Mixpanel for usage analysis
  • Customer Support Tools: Zendesk, Intercom for support ticket management

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes and government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Midasbuy, our users, or others
  • Prevent fraud, security breaches, or illegal activities

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption
  • PCI DSS Compliance: Payment card data handled according to PCI DSS Level 1 standards
  • Secure Storage: Personal data encrypted at rest in secure data centers
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access policies and authentication protocols
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response systems

Important: We never store complete credit card numbers. Payment data is tokenized and processed through certified payment gateways. We will never ask for your game account passwords.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality, authentication, and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze site usage and improve performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Object: Object to processing for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@midasbuy.com

Midasbuy is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@midasbuy.com, and we will delete such information from our systems.

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws.
